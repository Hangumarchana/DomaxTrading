About Domax Trading

Who are We ?

Domax Trading (Pvt) Ltd provides comprehensive electrical product solutions for your residential, office or industrial spaces, while taking every effort to make electrical energy usage safe and efficient. Founded in 2012 in Sri Lanka, Domax Trading sets its foot as innovative and rapidly expanding electrical items importing and distributing company.

Over the years, the company has earned a reputation for excellence and professionalism for continuously striving to meet – and even exceed – standards which set the climate for a sustainable business relationship with its clients.

As an electrical solution specialist, we offer electrical power products ranging from Plug Tops, Adapters, Sockets, Convertors, Extension Cords, Rechargeable Lights and even lighting fixtures. Our team is composed of technically skilled and highly dedicated personnel working cohesively to promote value- added solutions with a competitive pricing structure to suit your needs.
